ENCANTO MOVIE

Encanto is a 2021 American animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures.

ABOUT

An armed conflict forces the young couple Pedro and Alma Madrigal to flee their home village in Colombia with their infant triplets, Julieta, Pepa, and Bruno. Pedro is killed during their escape, but the candle Alma is carrying gains a magical power, repelling the attackers and creating Casita, a sentient house located in a magical realm bordered by mountains. Fifty years later, the village thrives under the candle's protection, which grants "gifts" to each Madrigal descendant when they turn five, enabling them to serve the villagers. However, ten years ago, Bruno, who was scapegoated for his ability to foresee troubling events, disappeared. Julieta's youngest daughter, 15-year-old Mirabel, inexplicably did not receive a gift, leaving her feeling like an outsider in her extraordinary family.

On his fifth birthday, Pepa's youngest son, Antonio, is granted the ability to communicate with animals. During the celebration, the other Madrigals unintentionally exclude Mirabel from the family photo. Mirabel notices cracks forming in Casita and the candle's flame flickering. She tries to warn her family, but Casita temporarily repairs itself, causing the Madrigals to dismiss her concerns. After overhearing Alma's prayers, Mirabel decides to save Casitaâ€™s magic herself. She talks to her sister Luisa, who, despite her superhuman strength, confesses she feels overwhelmed and that her gift has been faltering. Luisa suggests that the secret behind the fading magic might be in Bruno's room, a forbidden tower in Casita.
