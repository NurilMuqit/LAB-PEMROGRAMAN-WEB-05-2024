----------------- Bio -----------------
Mirai Kuriyama (栗山 未来, Kuriyama Mirai ) is the deuteragonist of Kyoukai no Kanata,
she's a first-year student and a member of the school's Literature Club.
Mirai comes from a bloodline of Spirit World Warriors who possess blood-manipulation abilities.
However, because these abilities are considered heretical among the Spirit World Warrior clans, she's shunned.
