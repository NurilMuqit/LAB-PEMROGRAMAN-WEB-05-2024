Mikasa's Description

ㅤㅤMikasa Ackerman is one of the main characters in the anime and manga series Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin), created by Hajime Isayama. She is a member of the Scout Regiment and is known for her incredible strength, combat skills, and deep loyalty to Eren Yeager, the protagonist of the series.

ㅤㅤMikasa is often portrayed as calm, stoic, and composed, especially in high-pressure situations. She rarely shows emotion except when it comes to her close friends, particularly Eren. Her loyalty to Eren stems from the fact that he saved her during a traumatic event in her childhood, and ever since then, she has felt deeply protective of him.
